About IUBAT

The International University of Business Agriculture and Technology (IUBAT) is the first non-government university in Bangladesh, established in 1991 by Dr. M Alimullah Miyan. Located in Uttara, Dhaka, IUBAT sits on a scenic 20-bigha (6.5-acre) green campus designed for sustainability and modern education. It is accredited by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and is a member of the Association of Commonwealth Universities. IUBAT offers a variety of undergraduate and graduate programs in disciplines such as engineering, business, agriculture, computer science, tourism, nursing, public health, and English. With around 8,000 students and over 260 faculty members, the university promotes a mission of “higher education for every qualified person,” regardless of socio-economic background. The campus features modern labs, a rich library, a medical center, sports facilities, and environmentally conscious infrastructure. It has been recognized globally for its green initiatives, ranking among the top sustainable universities in Bangladesh. IUBAT also supports research through its own institutes and focuses on the UN’s Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Committed to inclusivity, IUBAT provides scholarships, free transport, and health insurance for students, aiming to prepare ethical and skilled graduates for national and global development.
